<span id="handwave">👋🏻</span> Hi! I'm Ishneet, a Computer Engineering student at [Oxford](https://www.ox.ac.uk/). I do research at the [UW RSE Lab](https://rse-lab.cs.washington.edu/), work on robotics at [Menlo Research](#), and am a National Science Scholar at [A*STAR](https://www.a-star.edu.sg/).

My research interests lie broadly in machine learning — with a focus on LLMs, retrieval, reinforcement learning, robotics, computer vision, and interpretability.
